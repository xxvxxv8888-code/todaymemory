## English

### 1. Overview
"Today's Memory" is an **offline life-log app** that lets you record your day with photos, notes, region, and mood. The app has **no sign-up or login and never sends any data to any server.** All memories and photos you create are **stored only on your device.**

### 2. Information We Collect
The app **does not collect or transmit any personal information.** The developer cannot access any of your data.

- All information you enter (photos, notes, region name, dates, mood, receipts, cost, etc.) is stored **only in your device's local storage.**
- Location uses only an **administrative region name** that you choose or that is extracted from a receipt — never precise GPS location.
- Receipt text recognition (OCR) runs **entirely on-device**; no image or text is sent anywhere.
- The app uses no advertising, tracking, or analytics.

### 3. Permissions
- **Photo Library:** used to attach photos to your memories.
- **Camera:** used to take photos for your memories.
- Photos accessed via these permissions are stored only on your device and are never transmitted.

### 4. Data Retention & Deletion
- All data is stored on your device and is deleted when you delete the app.
- You can delete any memory at any time within the app.
- Backup files created by the "Backup" feature are stored only where you choose (Files app, AirDrop, etc.) and are managed solely by you.

### 5. Third Parties
The app does not share or sell any personal information to third parties.

### 6. Children's Privacy
Because the app collects no personal information, it is safe for all ages.
